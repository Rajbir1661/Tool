Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service really free to use?

Yes, our image compression tool is completely free to use with no hidden costs, watermarks, or registration required. We may show ads to support the free service, but you'll never pay to compress your images.

What image formats do you support?

We support all major image formats including JPEG, PNG, and WebP. You can compress these formats and also convert between them if needed. The original format will be used by default unless you choose a different output format.

Is there a file size limit?

You can compress images up to 20MB in size. For best performance with very large files, we recommend resizing them first or compressing in smaller batches if you have multiple images.

How do you protect my privacy?

All image processing happens directly in your web browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers or stored anywhere. This ensures complete privacy and security for your files.
